Scholorship 2025-26

Join ABIT Polytechnic for a Bright Future in Engineering and Technology

Important Instructions for Student :


  • 1. All the students are advised to observe office student section notice board for updates.

  • 2. The backward category students eligible for scholarship and freeship from The Commissioner, Social welfare should fill online application forms on mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in/ and submit the hardcopy of filled application form along with necessary documents to student section after display of corresponding notice.

  • 3. Physically handicapped students are eligible for getting scholarship from District Women-Child and Physically Handicapped Welfare Department.

  • 4. Minority students who have not taken any other fee concession are eligible for minority scholarship issued by Maharashtra State Government.

  • 5. All those students who have not applied for /eligible for any scholarship are eligible for meritorious students scholarship given by Maharashtra State Board Of Technical Education (MSBTE).The online application forms are available at MSBTE website.

  • Open category students belonging to economically backward class are eligible for following two scholarships:
    • i. Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Fee Reimbursement scheme.
    • ii. Dr. Punjabrao Deshmukh Livelihood allowance.
