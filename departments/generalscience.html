General Science Department

About General Science Department

The General Science department is dedicated to laying a strong academic foundation in physics, chemistry, and biology. Through a balanced blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, the department prepares students to excel in scientific reasoning and problem-solving. With lab-based learning, interdisciplinary projects, and research exposure, students are equipped to understand and address real-world scientific challenges.

Mr. Khandale Rahul Ramchandra
Head of Department

H.O.D's Message

Welcome to the Department of General Science at ABIT Polytechnic. Our department is dedicated to building a strong scientific foundation in students by nurturing curiosity and critical thinking. The program covers key areas of physics, chemistry, and biology, while encouraging exploration through hands-on experiments, collaborative projects, and real-world applications.

We aim to develop well-rounded individuals through academic excellence, scientific inquiry, and value-based education. I urge our students to stay inquisitive, think analytically, and use science as a tool to understand and improve the world around them.

Vision & Mission

Vision

  • Our aim at Abhaysinhraje Bhonsle Institute of Technology is to offer strong foundational education in science that inspires inquiry, innovation, and integrity. We are committed to empowering students from rural and semi-urban areas to become scientifically aware, ethically grounded, and socially responsible individuals capable of addressing global and local challenges.

Mission

  • To deliver high-quality, concept-driven science education that fosters curiosity, analytical thinking, and ethical responsibility. We strive to empower students—especially from rural backgrounds—with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to pursue advanced studies, contribute to scientific progress, and make informed contributions to society.

Goal

  • To become a leading center of excellence in science education and research — nurturing inquisitive minds from rural and semi-urban areas to develop critical thinking, environmental awareness, and ethical responsibility, while promoting a lifelong passion for discovery, innovation, and societal contribution.
