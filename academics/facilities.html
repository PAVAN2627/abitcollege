Our Facilities

Explore the world-class amenities we offer to enhance your college experience.

Library

The college library serves as a key information center, offering books, journals, and periodicals to support knowledge and learning. It ensures easy access to resources for students and staff, especially in Engineering and Technology, aiming to stay updated with the latest developments.

Bus

The college provides safe and affordable bus transportation for students and staff from Satara city and nearby areas like Karad, Umbraj, Masur. Bus services are expanded as student intake grows, ensuring smooth and convenient travel.

Hostel

The college hostel offers a comfortable and student-friendly environment with common rooms, indoor games, a reading area, and a small library—promoting social interaction and supporting academic growth.

Gym

The importance of exercise cannot be neglected for good physical and mental health. The institute offers gym facility to all its students and staff. The gym here has all the modern equipment for workout and is made available to students during morning and evening hours.

Computer Labs

A computer lab is a space which provides computer services to a defined community. Computer labs are typically provided by libraries to the public, by academic institutions to students who attend the institution, or by other institutions to the public or to people affiliated with that institution.

Playground

The college campus offers well-maintained playgrounds that provide students with the opportunity to engage in various outdoor sports and recreational activities. These facilities promote physical fitness, teamwork, and relaxation during their free time, contributing to a balanced college life.

Workshop

Workshop is main part of applied Engineering and considered as heart of the Mechanical department. For us Workshop means a place of worship, where we skill the hands. Workshop contains state-of-art equipment, qualified and experienced staff.

Canteen

The objective of the Canteen and meal service is to protect by reducing the risk of foodborne illness, with proper sanitary conditions, and preventing adulterated food. From the very beginning of the College in the College Canteen has been functioning efficiently.

Store

The college campus includes a stationery store that caters to the daily needs of students. It offers a wide range of study materials such as notebooks, pens, and academic supplies, along with essential consumer products for everyday use, ensuring convenience within the campus.

Xerox Center

Photo copying and printing facility is also available to cater to daily needs of students. Also spiral binding and project black book will be prepared at Xerox center.
