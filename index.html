About ABIT Polytechnic

Leading polytechnic institute under Vidyavardhini Charitable Trust, dedicated to excellence in technical education

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education and create skilled professionals who can contribute to the industrial and economic development of our nation.

Established under the Vidyavardhini Charitable Trust in Shahunagar Shendre, our institute has been at the forefront of technical education, combining traditional values with modern technology.
